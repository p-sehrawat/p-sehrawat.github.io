Scientific Notation:

Scientific notation simplifies representing and computing very large or small numbers by expressing them as a coefficient multiplied by a power of 10. For example, instead of writing 149,600,000 kilometers for the Earth-Sun distance, it can be expressed as 1.496 × 10^8 kilometers, making it easier to handle in calculations and comparisons.

Scientific Notation Practice:

  • 12,000,000
    1.2 x 10⁷
  • 0.0000032
    3.2 x 10⁻⁶
  • 8,500,000,000
    8.5 x 10⁹
